Satoru Iwata

Satoru Iwata demonstrating the 3D features of the Nintendo 3DS

  • 1959 - Satoru Iwata was born in Sapporo, Japan.
  • 1978 - Enrolled into Tokyo Institute of Technology with a major in computer science.
  • 1980 - Started working at the game development company, HAL Laboratory, Inc, to work as a part-time programmer.
  • 1982 - After graduation became the fifth full-time employee at HAL
  • 1983 - Became the company's corrdinator of software production and helped reach a deal with Nintendo to produce games for the Nintendo Entertainment Center.
  • 1983 - Became president of HAL and helped bring the company out of debt before bankruptcy.
  • 2000 - Joined Ninendo as the General Manager of the Corporate Planning Division.
  • 2002 - After the retirement of Hiroshi Yamauchi, Iwata becamse the fourth president of Nintendo.
  • 2004 - Introduced the Ninendo DS which became a huge success for the company.
  • 2006 Helped develop and release the Nintndo Wii which also became a huge success.
  • 2013 Became the CEO of Nintndo's American arm and conceptualized the Amiibo figurines
  • 2015 On July 11th, Satoru Iwata passes away due to complications from a tumor surgery he had the previous year.
